Home

  • Content: Welcome the user, hyperlinks to specific pages to enter main webpage
  • Only interaction and user input will be choosing which page to nagivate to
  • Notes: Page will not be accessable again once the user nagivgates to a specific page

About Madison

  • Purpose: This page is to inform the user about Madison, the book reviewer
  • Audience: Users who would like to know about the book reviewer Madison
  • Content: Facts, stories, and information pertaining to Madison
  • Actions: This page will not have any interactions from or with the user.
  • Notes: N/A

Favorite Books

  • Purpose: To give the user Madison's favorite book titles
  • Audience: Users who are interested in see what books are Madison's favorite
  • Content: Books that are loved by Madison, photos of these books for reference, reasons why the books are loved by Madison

Recommendations

Reviews
